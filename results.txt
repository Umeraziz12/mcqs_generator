Question 1: Who proposed the idea that matter is composed of tiny indivisible particles called atoms?
  A. John Dalton
  B. J.J. Thomson
  C. Democritus
  D. Erwin Schrödinger
Answer: Democritus

Question 2: What did J.J. Thomson discover in 1897?
  A. Protons
  B. Neutrons
  C. Electrons
  D. Canal rays
Answer: Electrons

Question 3: According to Dalton's atomic theory, what characterizes an atom?
  A. Atoms can be divided into smaller particles
  B. Atoms of the same element are different?
  C. All matter is made up of indivisible particles
  D. Atoms are made up of protons and electrons
Answer: All matter is made up of indivisible particles

Question 4: What did Goldstein discover in relation to subatomic particles?
  A. Neutrons
  B. Electrons
  C. Protons
  D. Isotopes
Answer: Protons

Question 5: Which experiment did Rutherford perform to investigate atomic structure?
  A. Cathode ray experiments
  B. Plum pudding experiment
  C. Gold foil experiment
  D. Canal ray experiments
Answer: Gold foil experiment
