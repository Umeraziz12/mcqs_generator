Question 1: Who proposed the atomic theory that stated 'all matter is made up of very small indivisible particles called atoms'?
  A. Democritus
  B. John Dalton
  C. J.J. Thomson
  D. Ernest Rutherford
Answer: John Dalton

Question 2: What did J.J. Thomson discover in 1897?
  A. Protons
  B. Neutrons
  C. Electrons
  D. Atoms
Answer: Electrons

Question 3: What type of particles did Goldstein discover in 1886?
  A. Electrons
  B. Neutrons
  C. Protons
  D. Alpha particles
Answer: Protons

Question 4: Who proposed the existence of neutrons and predicted their properties?
  A. Ernest Rutherford
  B. James Chadwick
  C. John Dalton
  D. J.J. Thomson
Answer: Ernest Rutherford

Question 5: In Rutherford's 'Gold Foil' experiment, what particles did he bombard the gold foil with?
  A. Beta particles
  B. Alpha particles
  C. Gamma rays
  D. Neutrons
Answer: Alpha particles

Question 4: What term refers to a piece of matter in pure form?

  B. Substance

Question 5: Which branch of chemistry focuses on the separation and analysis of samples to identify their components?
  A. Industrial Chemistry
